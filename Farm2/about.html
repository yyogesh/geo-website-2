We are a Scotland-based engineering consultancy on a mission to digitalise the way engineers work — from data management to full project collaboration.

Who We Are

A consultancy that
builds its own tools

Farm2 Engineering Consultancy Ltd is headquartered in Aberdeenshire, Scotland — the heart of the North Sea energy sector. We understand the real-world pressures of engineering projects because we've lived them.

That's why we built Ingeniator Lab — not as a generic project management tool, but as a precision-engineered platform that speaks the language of subsea, pipeline, and offshore engineers.

We believe that the combination of visualisation and collaboration is transformative for engineering teams. When everyone can see and interact with the same data simultaneously, decisions are faster, better-informed, and less costly to change.
